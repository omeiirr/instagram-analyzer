📝 How to Download Your Instagram Data

1. Access Settings

Open Instagram → Settings and Privacy → Accounts Center

2. Find Download Option

Your information → Download your information

3. Choose Settings

Select "Last 90 days" and "JSON" format

4. Submit & Wait

Submit request and wait for email (usually takes 1-2 days)

💡 Important

  • Select the "Followers and following" in the data options under "Connections"
  • Choose the 'Format' as JSON and not HMTL
  • For best results, select 'Date range' as 'All time'
  • You'll receive an email when your data is ready to download
